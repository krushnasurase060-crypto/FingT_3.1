🎵 What is FingT?

FingT is an interactive web application designed to help musicians of all levels train and improve their finger muscle memory. Whether you're a beginner guitarist, pianist, flutist, or drummer, FingT provides a comprehensive training environment with multiple game modes to strengthen your muscle memory and improve your performance.

With an intuitive interface, real-time feedback, and engaging training modes, FingT makes finger training enjoyable and effective.

🎯 Our Purpose

Our mission is to make finger training accessible, engaging, and effective for musicians worldwide. We believe that consistent muscle memory practice is fundamental to musical excellence, and FingT aims to make this practice enjoyable rather than tedious.

We're committed to continuous improvement, adding new features and training modes to keep your practice sessions fresh and motivating.

⚙️ How It Works

  • 1️⃣ Choose Your Mode — Select from Piano, Flute, Advanced, or Rhythm training
  • 2️⃣ Build Your Sequence — Click keys to create a training sequence
  • 3️⃣ Start Training — The app plays your sequence back, you repeat it
  • 4️⃣ Get Instant Feedback — Real-time feedback helps you improve
  • 5️⃣ Track Progress — Monitor your performance over time
